1.The Product

Our company “Heat-Up” is in the process of creating a new product which would be a heating portable and autonomous drink container. By buying this heating portable container you will be able to regulate the temperature of your drink and also benefit from a security temperature level (max temperature of 70 degrees Celsius), to avoid any customers betting burnt while drinking or carrying the product. The product itself would be a heating portable and autonomous drinks container but in addition a base with a battery inside which is the movable part of the product which you can take off for cleaning the product, recharge it, or if you only want a cold drink. This base’s purpose is to heat up your drink whenever you want and in any situation.
